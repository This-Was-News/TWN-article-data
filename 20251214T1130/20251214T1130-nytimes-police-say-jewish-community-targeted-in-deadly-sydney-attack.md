## Article summary
A rare mass shooting occurred at Sydney's Bondi Beach on Sunday, targeting a Jewish celebration for Hanukkah. At least 10 people were killed, including one of the suspected gunmen, and 11 others were injured, including two police officers. Authorities are investigating the incident as a terrorist attack. Witnesses described scenes of chaos as people fled the beach, with some hiding under barbecues and others scaling walls. The Australian Prime Minister condemned the attack, stating that an attack on Jewish Australians is an attack on every Australian. The Jewish community expressed deep pain, with reports indicating the event was specifically targeted. Police have stated the situation is neutralized with no active shooters unaccounted for. Bondi Beach, a famous and popular destination, was the scene of this tragic event. The motives of the gunmen are still under investigation, and authorities have established an exclusion zone as they examine suspicious items.
