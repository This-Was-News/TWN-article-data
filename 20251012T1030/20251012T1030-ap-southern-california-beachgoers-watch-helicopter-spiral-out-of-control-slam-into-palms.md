## Article summary
A helicopter lost control and crashed into a row of palms at Huntington Beach in Southern California. The incident occurred during a sunny day with beachgoers present. Videos show the helicopter spiraling before crashing near the beach and Pacific Coast Highway. Five people were hospitalized, including two from the helicopter and three bystanders. The helicopter was part of an annual Cars N Copters fundraising event. No cause for the crash has been released.
