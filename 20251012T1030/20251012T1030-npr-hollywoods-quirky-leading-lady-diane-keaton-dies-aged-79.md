## Article summary
Diane Keaton, the iconic actress known for her quirky roles and distinctive style, has passed away at the age of 79. Renowned for her Academy Award-winning performance in *Annie Hall*, Keaton remained a beloved figure in Hollywood for decades. She was celebrated for her unconventional fashion sense and endearing, eccentric characters on screen. Keaton's career spanned a wide range of roles, from comedic performances to dramatic parts in *The Godfather* trilogy. She also explored directing and writing, sharing her experiences with aging, love, and motherhood. Born in Los Angeles, Keaton pursued her dreams of performing, starting with Broadway and eventually making her mark in film. Her personal life included relationships with notable figures like Woody Allen and Warren Beatty, whom she supported despite controversies. Keaton never married but embraced an unconventional life, adopting two children later in life. Her legacy includes inspiring generations with her unique style and resilience, leaving behind a body of work that continues to captivate audiences.
