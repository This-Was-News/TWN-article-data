## Article summary
A devastating explosion at the Accurate Energetic Systems plant in rural Tennessee killed 16 people, with no survivors. The blast, felt miles around, left a smoldering wreck of twisted metal and burned-out vehicles. Authorities are investigating the cause, with no foul play ruled out yet. The community is in mourning, with vigils held to honor the victims. The plant supplies and researches explosives for the military. First responders are working methodically to recover remains and make the area safe. The company has faced past safety violations and was involved in a previous explosion in 2014. Residents reported feeling their homes shake and hearing a loud boom. Tennessee's governor has asked for prayers for the impacted families. The incident highlights the history of workplace accidents in the U.S.
