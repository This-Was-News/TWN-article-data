## Article summary
Saturday Night Live (SNL) has announced five new cast members ahead of its 51st season, including Ben Marshall, who transitions from the digital shorts team 'Please Don't Destroy' to a featured player. The new cast also includes Tommy Brennan, Jeremy Culhane, Kam Patterson, and Veronka Slowkowska. These additions follow recent departures of cast members Michael Longfellow, Devon Walker, and Eml Wakm, with Hed Gardner also not returning. SNL creator Lorne Michaels hinted at a post-50th anniversary shakeup. Ben Marshall, known for his work with Please Don't Destroy, expressed gratitude for his journey with the group. Tommy Brennan is a stand-up comedian with a growing online presence. Jeremy Culhane is an actor and writer recognized for his TikTok videos and work with Upright Citizens Brigade. Kam Patterson is a stand-up comedian known for his appearances on the podcast 'Kill Tony.' Veronka Slowkowska is an actress and comedian with a diverse background in comedy and acting, including roles in 'What We Do in the Shadows' and 'Tres.' The new season premieres on October 4.
