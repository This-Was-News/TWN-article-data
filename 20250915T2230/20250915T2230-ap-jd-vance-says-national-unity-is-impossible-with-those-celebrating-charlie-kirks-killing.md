## Article summary
Vice President JD Vance hosted Charlie Kirk's radio show, expressing his desire for national unity but stating it's impossible with those celebrating Kirk's killing. Vance, who was close to Kirk, recounted his conversations with Kirk's widow and criticized lies about Kirk that he blamed for the killing. He promised the Trump administration would act to stop political violence. Vance highlighted Kirk's role in boosting youth voter turnout and his close relationship with the Trump-Vance team. He also shared personal reflections on improving as a husband and father, inspired by Kirk's kindness. Other White House officials joined the show, discussing efforts to combat violence. Vance credited Kirk's influence in his political rise and the administration's success. After Kirk's death, Vance canceled his schedule to accompany Kirk's family and casket to Arizona.
