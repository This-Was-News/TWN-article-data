## Article summary
Body-worn camera footage captures police officers confronting Luigi Mangione inside a McDonald's restaurant in Altoona, Pennsylvania. This encounter occurred just five days after the fatal shooting of UnitedHealthcare CEO Brian Thompson in New York City. The video shows the officers approaching Mangione at the fast-food establishment.
