## Article summary
A federal judge allowed the deployment of National Guard units to Illinois, despite Governor JB Pritzker's claims of an unconstitutional invasion. President Trump threatened to use emergency powers to bypass courts and send troops. 200 Texas National Guard troops are set to arrive in Chicago. Legal battles and heated rhetoric continue between both sides. Trump considers invoking the Insurrection Act. Governor Pritzker vowed to fight the administration's plans. In Oregon, the Trump administration appealed a judge's order blocking troop deployments. Chicago's mayor plans to establish ICE-free zones. Broadview officials restricted protests at a federal facility. Trump described Chicago as a crime-ridden war zone, while his administration portrayed Portland as chaotic and violent.
