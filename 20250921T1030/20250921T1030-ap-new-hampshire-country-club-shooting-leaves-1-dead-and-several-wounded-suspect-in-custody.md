## Article summary
A shooting at the Sky Meadow Country Club in Nashua, New Hampshire, left one person dead and several others wounded. The deceased was an adult male, and the suspect, also an adult male, was detained at the scene. Initial reports of two shooters were incorrect. The motive remains unknown. Witnesses described a chaotic scene, with the shooter reportedly targeting someone and mentioning 'free Palestine.' Some victims' conditions are unknown. One witness is still searching for her son, who was reportedly injured. The shooter was described as wearing all black and a mask. Authorities assured there was no further danger to the public, and the mayor expressed confidence in the police investigation. U.S. Rep. Maggie Goodlander offered condolences to the victims and their families.
