## Article summary
Rob Reiner, the acclaimed actor and director, was dedicated to improving America, focusing on its perceived shortcomings. Shortly before his death, he shared his optimism about the nation's potential for betterment in an interview with journalist Todd Purdum. Reiner and his wife, Michelle Singer Reiner, were tragically found dead, and their son has been charged in their murders. Tributes to Reiner have highlighted not only his acting career and directorial work but also his significant political activism. Purdum's recent New York Times article details his final conversation with Reiner, offering insights into Reiner's views on his life's work and his perspective on America's future. This NPR "Consider This" episode features Purdum discussing these revelations with Scott Detrow. The episode was produced by Elena Burnett, edited by Courtney Dorning, and executive produced by Sami Yenigun.
