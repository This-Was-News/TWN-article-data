## Article summary
White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt announced on Instagram that she is expecting her second child, a baby girl, due in May. Leavitt, 28, shared her excitement about expanding her family and her son becoming a big brother. She is already a mother to a 1-year-old son with her husband, Nicholas Riccio. The White House has stated that Leavitt will continue in her role as press secretary after the birth of her second child. Leavitt has previously spoken about the challenges and rewards of balancing her high-profile career with motherhood, crediting her husband for his support. She views becoming a mother in the political world as providing valuable perspective. Leavitt, the youngest person to hold the press secretary position, often shares photos of her son on social media. She concluded her announcement by expressing gratitude to President Trump and Chief of Staff Susie Wiles for their support and for fostering a pro-family environment in the White House.
