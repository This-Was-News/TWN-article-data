## Article summary
Four people were killed and 10 wounded in a shooting that occurred during a family gathering at a banquet hall in Stockton, California. The victims included both children and adults. Sheriff's officials indicated that early indications suggest this may have been a targeted incident. The suspected shooter has not been apprehended, and authorities are seeking public assistance. Detectives are actively working to determine a motive for the shooting. The incident took place just before 6 p.m. inside the banquet hall, which is located in a shared parking lot with other businesses. Stockton, the city where the event occurred, has a population of 320,000 and is situated approximately 40 miles south of Sacramento. Local officials expressed sorrow over the tragedy, emphasizing that families should be able to gather safely. Further details regarding the conditions of the wounded victims have not been immediately released, though it was confirmed that several were transported to hospitals.
