## Article summary
The document is an indictment against John R. Bolton, former U.S. National Security Advisor, for allegedly mishandling classified information. Bolton is accused of sharing over a thousand pages of classified documents, including information up to the TOP SECRET/SCI level, with two unauthorized individuals via personal email and messaging apps. He also retained classified documents in his home after his tenure ended. The indictment details Bolton's actions from April 2018 to September 2019, including sending diary-like entries containing classified information. The document also mentions a hack of Bolton's personal email by a cyber actor associated with Iran, which compromised the classified information. Bolton's public statements on the importance of handling classified information properly are also highlighted. The indictment includes charges for transmission and retention of national defense information, with a notice of forfeiture upon conviction.
