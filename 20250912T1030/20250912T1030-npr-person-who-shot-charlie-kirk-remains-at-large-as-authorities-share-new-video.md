## Article summary
The FBI has released photos of a person of interest in the fatal shooting of conservative activist and podcaster Charlie Kirk at Utah Valley University. Kirk, the 31-year-old CEO of Turning Point USA, was shot while speaking at the first stop of his American Comeback Tour. President Trump announced his death and ordered U.S. flags to be flown at half-staff. The Utah Department of Public Safety believes the shooting was a targeted attack, but the shooter's identity and motive remain unknown. The FBI is offering a $100,000 reward for information leading to the arrest of the responsible individual(s). Investigators have tracked the suspect's movements and recovered a high-powered rifle near the scene. Vice President Vance and second lady Usha Vance will visit Salt Lake City to pay respects to Kirk's family. Trump plans to posthumously award Kirk the Presidential Medal of Freedom. The university confirmed Kirk was shot at 12:10 p.m. local time, and the suspect is believed to be of college age. Authorities are asking for the public's help in identifying the shooter and have received numerous tips.
