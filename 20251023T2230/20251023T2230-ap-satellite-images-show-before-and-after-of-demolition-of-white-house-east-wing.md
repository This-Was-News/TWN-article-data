## Article summary
New satellite images from Planet Labs PBC reveal the demolition of the White House East Wing as President Donald Trump proceeds with plans to construct a new ballroom. The images, taken on September 26 and October 23, 2025, show the significant changes to the White House's structure. The East Wing, which was intact in the earlier image, has been demolished in the later one. This demolition is part of Trump's initiative to expand and renovate the White House. The images provide a clear visual representation of the ongoing construction and the scale of the changes being made. The White House East Wing, a significant part of the presidential residence, is being replaced to accommodate the new ballroom. The satellite images offer a unique perspective on the transformation of one of the most recognizable buildings in the world. The demolition and construction process is a testament to the ongoing efforts to modernize and expand the White House facilities. The images highlight the progress made in a short period, showcasing the rapid pace of the construction work. This initiative is part of a broader plan to enhance the White House's functionality and aesthetic appeal.
