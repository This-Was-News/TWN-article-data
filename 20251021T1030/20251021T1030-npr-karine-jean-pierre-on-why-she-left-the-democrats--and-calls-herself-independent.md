## Article summary
Karine Jean-Pierre, former White House press secretary, reflects on her time in the Biden administration and her decision to leave the Democratic Party in her new book, *Independent: A Look Inside a Broken White House, Outside the Party Lines*. She discusses the party's internal issues and disloyalty, which she believes contributed to their 2024 defeat. Jean-Pierre shares her observations from the 2024 presidential debate, noting concerns about President Biden's health but maintaining her support for his ability to serve. She criticizes the Democrats' inability to unite behind Biden, citing examples of prominent figures who expressed doubts. Jean-Pierre also addresses the unique challenges she faced as a Black woman in her role and the importance of representation. She hopes her book will spark conversations about the future of American politics and encourage civic engagement.
