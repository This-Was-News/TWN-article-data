## Article summary
A mass shooting at Willies Bar and Grill on St. Helena Island, South Carolina, left four people dead and at least 20 injured during a high school alumni event. The bar, a community pillar serving Gullah-inspired cuisine, was packed when the shooting occurred. Bar owner Willie Turral described scenes of screaming and panic as people sought shelter. The Beaufort County Sheriffs Office is investigating the incident, with four victims in critical condition. The Gullah community, with roots tracing back to enslaved West Africans, is known for preserving African heritage. U.S. Rep. Nancy Mace expressed heartbreak over the shooting. The incident has left the community in shock and mourning.
