## Article summary
The 2025 Emmy Awards featured notable moments beyond Seth Rogen's record-breaking wins with 'The Studio.' Owen Cooper, 15, became the youngest male acting winner for 'Adolescence,' encouraging viewers to step out of their comfort zones. Stephen Colbert's 'The Late Show' won its first best talk series award despite its upcoming cancellation. Tramell Tillman made history as the first Black actor to win in his category for 'Severance,' dedicating his award to his mother. Noah Wyle won his first Emmy for 'The Patient' after years of nominations. The ceremony avoided major political controversies, with hosts and winners adhering to speech time limits to raise funds for charity. The in memoriam segment honored late stars like Malcolm Jamal Warner, with notable absences including Hulk Hogan. The Emmys celebrated anniversaries of shows like 'The Golden Girls,' 'Survivor,' 'Gilmore Girls,' 'Law & Order,' and 'Grey's Anatomy,' with emotional tributes and reunions.
