## Article summary
Erika Kirk, the wife of conservative activist Charlie Kirk, reflects on her husband's life and death. Charlie Kirk, founder of Turning Point USA, was shot during a campus debate. Erika recalls his excitement before the event and his final words. She describes their strong Christian marriage and his impact on her life. After his death, Erika struggles with grief but finds solace in her faith. She plans to continue her husband's work at Turning Point USA. Erika also discusses her decision not to seek revenge against the man who killed her husband, emphasizing her belief in divine justice. She views her husband's death as part of God's plan and finds peace in surrendering to it.
