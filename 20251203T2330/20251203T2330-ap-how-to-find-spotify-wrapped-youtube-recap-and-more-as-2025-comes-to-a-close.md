## Article summary
As 2025 draws to a close, various platforms are releasing their annual recaps, offering users a look back at their digital activities throughout the year. Spotify's Wrapped, available since December 3rd, highlights top songs, artists, and podcasts, and introduces a new feature called Wrapped Party for comparing listening habits with friends. YouTube Recap, launched on December 2nd, provides an overview of watch history, including top music artists and songs, accessible via the YouTube homepage or the 'You' tab. Apple Music's Replay, also released on December 2nd, details top songs, artists, and genres, found in the 'Home' tab under 'Your Top Music' or on replay.music.apple.com. Amazon Music's annual recap, 'Delivered,' rolled out on December 2nd for customers with sufficient listening history, accessible through the 'Find' button in the Amazon Music App. Twitch offers a recap for viewers and streamers who have logged at least 10 hours of content, available at twitch.tv/annual-recap. Additionally, the language-learning app Duolingo released its 'year in review' in early December, showcasing user progress in lessons, streaks, and time spent on the app, which can be accessed via a logo on the home navigation page. Most platforms recommend updating the app to the latest version for the best experience.
