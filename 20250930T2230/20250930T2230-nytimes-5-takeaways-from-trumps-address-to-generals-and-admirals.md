## Article summary
President Trump addressed top military leaders at a gathering in Quantico, Virginia, where he and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth delivered speeches covering various policy issues. The event, unusual for its assembly of global military commanders, saw Trump discussing topics beyond military matters. He highlighted his domestic and foreign policy moves, including the deployment of the National Guard to American cities and his relationship with Russian President Vladimir Putin. Trump's remarks also touched on nuclear weapons and other cultural issues from a right-wing perspective. The audience of generals and admirals listened impassively as the president and secretary laid out their talking points in a campaign-style manner.
