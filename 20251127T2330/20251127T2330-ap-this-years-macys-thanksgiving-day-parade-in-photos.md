## Article summary
The holiday season commenced with the annual Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade in New York, featuring a vibrant display of massive helium balloons. Spectators braved chilly weather to witness beloved characters and new additions float through the city streets. Among the highlights were balloons depicting Buzz Lightyear, Nintendo's Mario, and Pac-Man, joining traditional favorites. The parade route, including Sixth Avenue and Central Park West, was lined with enthusiastic crowds. Floats, such as the Tom Turkey float and Santa's Sleigh, also made their way through the parade. Various popular characters like Shrek, Goku, Sponge Bob, and Disney's Minnie Mouse were represented as balloons. Other notable balloons included Diary of a Wimpy Kid, Monkey D. Luffy, Stuart the Minion, and Bluey. The event showcased a festive atmosphere with confetti filling the air and balloon handlers expertly guiding the colossal figures. The parade marked the traditional start of the festive season, bringing joy to onlookers both on the streets and from apartment windows.
