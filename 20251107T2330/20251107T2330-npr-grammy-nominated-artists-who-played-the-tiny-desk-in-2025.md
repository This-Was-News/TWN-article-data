## Article summary
The NPR Tiny Desk series has once again proven to be a significant platform for artists, with many musicians who performed in 2025 receiving 2026 Grammy nominations. This highlights the intimate and talent-showcasing nature of the Tiny Desk concerts, where artists perform without monitors or backing tracks, fostering a direct connection with their audience. Among the nominated artists who graced the Tiny Desk stage are Kendrick Lamar, who leads all nominees with nine nods, and the reunited hip-hop duo Clipse, who earned five nominations. Other notable Tiny Desk alumni receiving nominations include Bad Bunny, Leon Thomas, Turnstile, Tame Impala, PinkPantheress, Wet Leg, Bilal, Michael Mayo, Buena Vista Social Club, Death Becomes Her, Gypsy, Sierra Hull, CeCe Winans, Grupo Frontera, Carín León, and Gloria Estefan. The article also points out the unique instance of Leon Thomas' song "Mutt" being nominated for best R&B performance and Ca7riel & Paco Amoroso's album "PAPOTA," which includes music recorded at Tiny Desk in 2024, receiving a nomination for best Latin rock or alternative album. This widespread recognition underscores the Tiny Desk's role as a tastemaker in the music industry, consistently featuring artists who go on to achieve critical acclaim and award recognition.
