## Article summary
Charlie Kirk, a prominent conservative activist and founder of Turning Point USA, was fatally shot while speaking at Utah Valley University. The incident sparked outrage and grief across the political spectrum, with figures from both parties condemning the violence. Initial reports suggested a suspect was in custody, but authorities later clarified that the detained individual was not the shooter. Videos from the scene showed chaos and panic, with witnesses describing the moment Kirk was struck. The event was part of Kirk's 'American Comeback Tour,' aimed at engaging college campuses. Kirk, a close ally of former President Trump, was known for his influential role in conservative youth activism. The shooting prompted an outpouring of condolences and calls for unity, with Trump ordering flags to be flown at half-staff in Kirk's honor. Investigations are ongoing, and the motive remains unclear.
