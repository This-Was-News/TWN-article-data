## Article summary
Charlie Kirk, the CEO and co-founder of the conservative youth organization Turning Point USA, has died after being shot during an event at Utah Valley University. President Donald Trump confirmed the news on Truth Social, calling Kirk a great and legendary figure. Kirk was speaking at a debate hosted by TPUSA when he was fatally shot. The event had sparked divided opinions on campus, with an online petition calling for his appearance to be barred. Videos from the scene show Kirk being shot in the neck, with stunned spectators reacting in shock. The campus has been evacuated, and the suspect remains at large. Kirk's death follows a series of political violence incidents in the United States. Supporters have gathered at the Turning Point USA headquarters in Phoenix, expressing their grief. Political figures from both parties have condemned the shooting, emphasizing the need to stand against political violence. Kirk was known for his conservative activism and close ties to President Trump.
