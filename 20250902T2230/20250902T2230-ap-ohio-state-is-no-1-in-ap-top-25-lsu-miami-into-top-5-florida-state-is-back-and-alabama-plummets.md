## Article summary
Ohio State has claimed the No. 1 spot in the AP Top 25 college football poll, receiving 55 of 66 first-place votes after their win over Texas. LSU and Miami have moved into the top five, while Florida State has re-entered the rankings at No. 14, causing Alabama to plummet to No. 21, its lowest ranking in 17 seasons. Texas dropped to No. 7 following a tumultuous Labor Day weekend where four top-10 teams lost. Penn State remains at No. 2, and Oregon, Clemson, Notre Dame, and South Carolina round out the top 10. Utah is the only new entrant at No. 25, while Boise State and Kansas State have dropped out. The SEC leads with 10 ranked teams, followed by the Big Ten with six. Notable upcoming matchups include No. 15 Michigan vs. No. 18 Oklahoma.
