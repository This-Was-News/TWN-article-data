## Article summary
YouTube's 2025 annual trends report highlights a mix of contemporary hits and nostalgic throwbacks, with Connie Francis' 1962 song "Pretty Little Baby" achieving viral status. The report features songs from the hit anime movie *KPop Demon Hunters* alongside contemporary artists like Doechii and Billie Eilish. YouTube also launched its first personalized "YouTube Recap" service for 2025, allowing users to review their individual viewing habits. Trending topics for the year include the Netflix drama *Squid Game*, Labubu soft toys, and user-generated content on Roblox, such as the game *Grow a Garden*. The platform's trends also indicate the popularity of conservative political views, with podcasts by Joe Rogan and Shawn Ryan featuring in the top 10. Influencer Charlie Kirk was recognized on both the top U.S. creators and trending topics lists. Music trends include multiple entries from Kendrick Lamar and Bruno Mars, with Bruno Mars collaborating with Lady Gaga and Rosé. The song "APT" by Rosé, featuring user-generated content, became the fastest KPop music video to reach 1 billion views on YouTube.
