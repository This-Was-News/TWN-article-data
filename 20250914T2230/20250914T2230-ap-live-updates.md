## Article summary
The 77th Emmy Awards are taking place on CBS and streaming on Paramount+, hosted by comedian Nate Bargatze at the Peacock Theater in Los Angeles. The event features stars from top shows like 'Adolescence,' 'The Penguin,' 'Severance,' and 'The Studio.' Apple CEO Tim Cook attended to support 'Severance' and 'The Studio,' both Apple TV+ productions, highlighting the show's cultural influence. Notable attendees include Justine Lupe, Sterling K. Brown, and Walton Goggins. The Emmys are expected to be dominated by shows like 'Severance' and 'The Studio,' which have already won multiple Creative Arts Emmys. The ceremony will award 25 Prime Time Emmys, with the rest given during the Creative Arts ceremony. Kathy Bates and Jean Smart are predicted to win in their respective acting categories. The event will be broadcast live on CBS and streamed on Paramount+, with additional content available later for standard subscribers.
