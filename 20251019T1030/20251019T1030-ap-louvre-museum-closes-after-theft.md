## Article summary
The Louvre Museum in Paris was forced to close after a daring heist on Sunday. Thieves used a basket lift and a freight elevator to gain access to the Apollo Gallery, where they stole nine priceless jewels from the collection of Napoleon and the Empress. The operation lasted just seven minutes, indicating a well-planned operation. The museum's closure caused chaos among tourists, with police securing the area.
