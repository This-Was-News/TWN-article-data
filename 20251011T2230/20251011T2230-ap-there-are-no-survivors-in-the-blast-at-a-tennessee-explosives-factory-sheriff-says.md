## Article summary
A devastating explosion at an explosives plant in rural Tennessee left no survivors and caused widespread damage. Authorities confirmed that 16 people were missing, and the cause of the blast remains unknown. Sheriff Chris Davis expressed grief and asked for prayers for the victims' families. A vigil was held at Hurricane Chapel, where community members came together to pray and support each other. The explosion destroyed the Accurate Energetic Systems plant, which supplies explosives for the military. Rescue teams are working cautiously due to the volatile nature of the site. The community is deeply affected, with many knowing the victims personally. State officials brought in a rapid DNA team to identify the remains. The explosion was felt miles away, with residents reporting their homes shaking. Governor Bill Lee asked for prayers for the impacted families. The company has a history of safety violations and a previous explosion in 2014.
