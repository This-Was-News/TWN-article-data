## Article summary
A ceasefire between Israel and Hamas has led to the release of over 1,900 Palestinian prisoners in exchange for Israeli hostages. Families in Gaza and Israel are reuniting amid the devastation. Humanitarian aid is beginning to enter Gaza, and displaced Palestinians are returning to their homes. The ceasefire, brokered by President Trump, has seen Israeli forces pull back to agreed-upon lines in Gaza. Photos show scenes of celebration and mourning, as well as the ongoing challenges faced by those affected by the conflict. The ceasefire has brought a mix of hope and hardship, with efforts to rebuild and recover underway.
