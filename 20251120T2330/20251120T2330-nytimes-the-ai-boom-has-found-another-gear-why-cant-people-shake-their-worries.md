## Article summary
The tech industry is experiencing a period of unprecedented growth, with companies like Nvidia reporting historic profits and achieving trillion-dollar valuations. Nvidia's quarterly profit soared to nearly $32 billion, a significant increase from previous years, and the company briefly became the first to reach a $5 trillion market value. Major tech players such as Microsoft, Google, Apple, and Amazon also boast trillion-dollar valuations and substantial combined profits. Despite this apparent boom, some industry insiders express concern, viewing the rapid expansion and wealth creation as potentially unsustainable, akin to a "house of cards." They suggest that the high demand for Nvidia's chips may not directly translate to widespread AI adoption, but rather to the development of large AI systems in anticipation of future use. This underlying anxiety was reflected in a brief market downturn, with Nvidia's stock experiencing a dip and the broader S&P 500 index falling.
