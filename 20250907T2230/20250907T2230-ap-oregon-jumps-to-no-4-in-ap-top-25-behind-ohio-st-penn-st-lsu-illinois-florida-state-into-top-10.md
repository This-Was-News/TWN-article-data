## Article summary
Oregon has risen to the No. 4 spot in the AP Top 25 college football poll, behind Ohio State, Penn State, and LSU. Ohio State remains at the top with 57 first-place votes after a dominant win over Grambling. Penn State and LSU follow in the second and third positions. Oregon's victory over Oklahoma State has propelled them back into the top five, while South Florida has entered the rankings for the first time in seven years. Notable movements include Oklahoma's jump to No. 13, Tennessee's rise to No. 15, and South Florida's significant ascent to No. 18 after upsetting then-No. 13 Florida. Auburn and Missouri have also cracked the rankings, while Arizona State, Florida, and SMU have fallen out. The Southeastern Conference leads with 11 ranked teams, followed by the Big Ten with six. Key upcoming matchups include South Florida at No. 5 Memphis, Georgia at Tennessee, and Texas A&M at Notre Dame.
