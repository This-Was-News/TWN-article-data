## Article summary
The web page showcases the top photos of the day selected by Associated Press photo editors, featuring a range of significant and powerful moments. Highlights include Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan addressing the United Nations General Assembly, protests in New York during the same event, and a rally displaying faces of individuals allegedly killed by the Iranian government. Additionally, the gallery features a construction worker in a metro tunnel beneath the Panama Canal, the clearing of a homeless encampment in Los Angeles, and armed demonstrators supporting Venezuelan President Nicolas Maduro. Sports enthusiasts can see a moment from a baseball game between the St. Louis Cardinals and the San Francisco Giants. The page also includes a ceremonial event at the Ise Jingu shrine complex in Japan, an Ultra-Orthodox Jewish man praying in Jerusalem, and the premiere of a short film in Milan. Nature lovers can enjoy a photo of storks flying over fields in Germany. The page encourages followers to engage with AP's visual journalism through their photography website and Instagram.
