## Article summary
The web page discusses Alexandre de Moraes, a Brazilian Supreme Court justice known as 'Big Alex,' who led the trial of former President Jair Bolsonaro for orchestrating a coup attempt. De Moraes, recognized for his bold actions, sentenced Bolsonaro to 27 years in prison, despite U.S. pressure and sanctions. The trial also resulted in sentences for Bolsonaro's allies, including military officials. De Moraes, once seen as conservative, is now praised by some and criticized by others, including Elon Musk and Bolsonaro supporters. His role in protecting Brazil's democratic institutions and electoral integrity has been significant, though controversial. The page highlights his unyielding stance on duty and the polarizing reactions to his decisions.
