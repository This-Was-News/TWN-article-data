## Article summary
The article discusses the complex timeline and factors leading to the hostage deal and potential cease-fire between Israel and Hamas. It highlights President Trump's shift in approach, initially allowing Israel's offensive without guardrails, then pushing for a deal. The article explores missed opportunities for earlier negotiations, the impact of Hamas's leadership crisis, and the role of Trump's unorthodox diplomacy. It also mentions the influence of key figures like Jared Kushner and the strategic calculations of Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu. The article concludes by noting the public's overwhelming support for bringing hostages home, despite the diplomatic and humanitarian costs of the prolonged war.
