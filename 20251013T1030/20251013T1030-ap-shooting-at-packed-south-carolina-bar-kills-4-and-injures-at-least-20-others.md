## Article summary
A mass shooting at Willies Bar and Grill on St. Helena Island, South Carolina, has left four people dead and at least 20 injured. The bar was hosting a high school alumni event when the shooting occurred, causing panic and fear among the crowd. The Beaufort County Sheriffs Office is investigating the incident, with four victims in critical condition. Bar owner Willie Turral described the scene as chaotic, with people seeking shelter from the gunfire. The bar is known for its Gullah-inspired cuisine and community involvement. St. Helena Island is home to a large Gullah community, descendants of enslaved West Africans who worked rice plantations in the area. The shooting has left the community and officials devastated, with U.S. Rep. Nancy Mace expressing her heartbreak over the incident.
