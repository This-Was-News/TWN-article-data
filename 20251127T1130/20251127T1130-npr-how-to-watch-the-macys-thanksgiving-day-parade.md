## Article summary
The 99th annual Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade will take place on Thursday, November 27th, starting at 8:30 a.m. in all time zones. Viewers can watch the parade live on NBC and Peacock, with a Spanish-language simulcast available on Telemundo. The parade will feature over 5,000 volunteers, 34 balloons, 28 floats, and 11 marching bands. Musical performances will include stars like Cynthia Erivo, Darlene Love, Ciara, and the cast of "KPop Demon Hunters." Broadway shows such as "Buena Vista Social Club" and "Ragtime" will also perform, along with a special centennial performance by The Rockettes. This year's parade will reintroduce historic balloon elements like Rainbow and Greybow Trouts, alongside newcomers such as Buzz Lightyear and PAC-MAN. Familiar favorites like Snoopy and SpongeBob SquarePants will also be present. Hoda Kotb, Savannah Guthrie, and Al Roker will host the event. For those eager to get a sneak peek, NBC and Peacock will air a primetime special, "Countdown to the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade," on Wednesday, November 26th. The parade route begins on West 77th Street and Central Park West, concluding at Macy's Herald Square.
