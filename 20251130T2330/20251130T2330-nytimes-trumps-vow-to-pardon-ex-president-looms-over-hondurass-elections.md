## Article summary

The election has been influenced by President Trump's endorsement of a candidate and his announcement of a pardon for a former president convicted of drug trafficking. Two right-wing candidates, including the one backed by Trump, have raised concerns about Honduras becoming a socialist-led authoritarian state, drawing parallels to Venezuela. They have also accused the current left-wing party of plotting to retain power.
