## Article summary
Associated Press photographers have captured a diverse array of images showcasing Christmas celebrations across the globe. From the traditional birthplace of Jesus in Bethlehem to bustling cities like Beijing and Jakarta, the holiday spirit is evident. In Northern Ireland, brave swimmers took to the cold sea for a charity event, while in Germany, people gathered for bell ringing ceremonies and tractor parades. Indonesia saw singers filling a stadium for a Christmas Eve service, and in Myanmar, Christians attended prayer at St. Mary Cathedral. The West Bank city of Bethlehem was a focal point, with processions and the arrival of the Latin Patriarch. Celebrations also extended to Lebanon, where children marked Christmas Eve near the Israeli border. London hosted its annual Christmas Eve meat auction, and Ukrainian servicemen participated in parades, with citizens singing carols in Kyiv's subway stations. In Nazareth, Israel, children in Santa costumes watched a Christmas parade. The collection highlights the global reach of Christmas, marked by joy, reverence, and light.
