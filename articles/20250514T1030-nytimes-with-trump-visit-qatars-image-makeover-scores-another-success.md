## Article summary
The visit of President Trump to Qatar, hosted by Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al-Thani, marks a significant turnaround in the Gulf emirate's international image, previously tarnished by accusations of terrorism funding. As part of this charm offensive, Qatar is reportedly considering gifting Trump a luxury jetliner, which has sparked backlash from both Democrats and some of Trump's own supporters. Critics question the ethics of accepting such a gift, especially given Qatar's controversial role in regional conflicts, including its ties to Hamas. Despite the criticisms, Qatar has worked to enhance its global standing, notably through hosting the FIFA World Cup in 2022 and maintaining crucial communication channels during conflicts.
