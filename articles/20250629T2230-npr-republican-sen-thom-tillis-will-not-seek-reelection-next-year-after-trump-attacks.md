## Article summary
Republican Senator Thom Tillis of North Carolina announced he will not seek reelection next year, following criticism from President Trump over his vote against a GOP tax and spending bill. Tillis was one of the few Senate Republicans to oppose the legislation, citing concerns about its Medicaid provisions and potential costs to his state. In his statement, he expressed frustration with the lack of bipartisanship in Washington, criticizing politicians who prioritize political gain over the welfare of their constituents. He reflected on the divisive political atmosphere and chose to focus on family instead of enduring another term in office.
