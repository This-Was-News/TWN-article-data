## Article summary
Forbes has released its 2025 billionaires list, revealing a record 3,028 billionaires worldwide, who collectively hold approximately $16.1 trillion in wealth, an increase of $2 trillion from the previous year. The United States leads with 902 billionaires, followed by China and Hong Kong with 516, and India with 205. The top ten billionaires include Elon Musk, with a net worth of $342 billion, followed by Mark Zuckerberg, Jeff Bezos, and Larry Ellison. The list is compiled by a team of over 100 reporters who assess various assets, investments, and expenditures of the billionaires. Public attitudes towards billionaires have shifted, influenced by movements like Occupy Wall Street and political changes, leading to increased scrutiny of their wealth and power. Despite some negative perceptions, billionaires are noted to be more influential than ever. The full list can be accessed through Forbes' website.
