## Article summary
A shooting spree in Kentucky on Sunday resulted in the deaths of two women and injuries to three others, including a state trooper. The incident began when the trooper pulled over a vehicle near the Blue Grass Airport, leading to a carjacking and subsequent shootings at the Richmond Road Baptist Church. The shooter, who may have had connections to the church, was fatally shot by police. The trooper and two men were wounded, with one man in critical condition. Governor Andy Beshear expressed condolences and urged support for the affected community.
