## Article summary
President Trump hosted an exclusive dinner at his Virginia golf club, attended by hundreds of high-paying supporters of his personal cryptocurrency, $TRUMP. The event aimed to promote the crypto industry and was marked by protests outside, accusing Trump of corrupting the presidency. Guests included international investors eager to influence U.S. financial regulations, with Trump criticizing the Biden administration's actions against crypto companies. The dinner was organized by Trump's business partners, framing it as a contest where the top buyers of $TRUMP coins could dine with him, with substantial fees generated from the cryptocurrency transactions. Notable attendees included billionaire Justin Sun and various crypto executives hoping to gain insights into future regulations. The dinner menu featured gourmet dishes and was accompanied by Trump’s promises to support the crypto community. Critics, including Senator Jeff Merkley, condemned the event as blatant corruption. Despite the controversy, the White House defended Trump's actions, asserting he was not profiting from the presidency. Overall, the gala underscored Trump’s ongoing ties to the cryptocurrency market and his family's financial interests.
