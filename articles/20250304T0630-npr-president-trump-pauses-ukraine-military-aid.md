## Article summary
President Trump has paused military aid to Ukraine following a contentious meeting with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. The decision to halt aid comes as the Trump administration reviews its support to ensure it aligns with peace efforts. During the meeting, tensions escalated as Trump accused Zelenskyy of "gambling with World War III" and disrespecting the U.S. Zelenskyy was seeking security guarantees from the U.S. to prevent further Russian territorial gains, a commitment Trump has been hesitant to make.
