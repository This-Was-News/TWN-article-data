## Article summary
At the NCAA Division I wrestling championship in Philadelphia, President Trump was warmly received by the crowd, while reactions to Elon Musk were more mixed. Many attendees expressed their support for Trump but shared complicated feelings about Musk's involvement in his administration. Some fans criticized Musk's business decisions and felt his influence made Trump appear weak. Despite acknowledging Musk's contributions to Trump's election, several supporters questioned the appropriateness of his presence at such events. Attendees voiced concerns about Musk's role in the administration, particularly regarding recent job cuts at the Department of Agriculture. While some attendees trusted Trump to navigate these issues, others felt Musk's constant presence was unwelcome and detracted from Trump's image. The atmosphere at the event highlighted a divide among Trump's supporters regarding Musk's influence. Overall, the wrestling championship served as a backdrop for the evolving dynamics between Trump, Musk, and their supporters.
