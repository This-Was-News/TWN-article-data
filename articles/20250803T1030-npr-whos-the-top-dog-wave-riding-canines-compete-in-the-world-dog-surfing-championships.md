## Article summary
The World Dog Surfing Championships took place on August 2, 2025, at Pacifica State Beach, California, attracting thousands of spectators. Around 15 to 20 dogs, including Charlie, a 10-year-old yellow lab, competed in various heats, with some surfing tandem or with their owners. Judges evaluated the dogs based on their balance, duration on the board, and tricks performed. Iza, a 5-year-old French bulldog, won her heat, while Charlie and his "Dream Team" also competed. The event highlighted the unique bond between dogs and their owners, with winners receiving medals and bragging rights.
