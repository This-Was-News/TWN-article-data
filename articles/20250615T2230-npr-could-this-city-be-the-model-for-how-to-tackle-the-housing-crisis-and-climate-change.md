## Article summary

These dwellings are designed with climate-friendly features such as solar panels, thick insulation, and communal green spaces, which contribute to energy efficiency and comfort year-round. The success of Vienna's social housing lies in its ability to provide quality living conditions across different income levels, fostering a sense of community among residents.
