## Article summary
Rama Duwaji, the wife of Democratic mayoral primary candidate Zohran Mamdani, gained attention as he celebrated his likely victory in the race for New York City mayor. Duwaji, 27, is an accomplished animator and illustrator whose work has been featured in prominent publications like The New Yorker and BBC. Born in Texas to Syrian parents, she holds a master's degree in illustration from the School of Visual Arts in New York. Throughout the campaign, Mamdani engaged younger voters with his charismatic social media presence, while Duwaji showcased her artistic talents to over 70,000 Instagram followers, often focusing on Middle Eastern life and social justice issues. Despite her limited public appearances during the campaign, Mamdani defended her visibility, emphasizing her individuality as an artist. The couple, who met through a dating app, married in a civil ceremony this winter and celebrated with a nikkah in Dubai. On election day, Duwaji expressed her pride in her husband through a heartfelt Instagram post, sharing personal moments and their journey together.
