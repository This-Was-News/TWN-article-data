## Article summary

As the first American pope, Cardinal Robert Francis Prevost emphasizes his diverse background and has expressed disapproval of Trump's hardline policies, particularly on immigration and climate change. Critics and supporters alike note that Pope Leo's advocacy for migrants could challenge Trump's aggressive deportation efforts. While some Trump supporters appreciate the pope's anti-abortion stance, others have labeled him anti-Trump and pro-open borders. 

Pope Leo's American identity was not a focal point in his selection, with Vatican officials emphasizing a desire to strengthen the Christian faith rather than political affiliations.
