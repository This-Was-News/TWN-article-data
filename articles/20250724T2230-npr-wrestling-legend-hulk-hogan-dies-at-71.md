## Article summary
Wrestling legend Hulk Hogan, born Terry Bollea, has died at the age of 71 following a cardiac arrest. Hogan was a prominent figure in professional wrestling, particularly during the 1980s, and was a major star for World Wrestling Entertainment. Known for his immense popularity, Hogan's era was dubbed "Hulkamania," and he also ventured into acting, including a role in "Rocky III." In recent years, Hogan became associated with right-wing politics, notably supporting Donald Trump at the 2024 Republican National Convention. Additionally, Hogan gained media attention after winning a lawsuit against Gawker Media, which published a portion of a sex tape, resulting in a $140 million award that led to Gawker's bankruptcy.
