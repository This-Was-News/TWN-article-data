## Article summary
The article describes the aftermath of devastating floods in Kerrville, Texas, where the city has become a hub for search and recovery efforts. While much of Kerrville escaped severe damage, some areas, particularly near the Guadalupe River, were heavily affected, with homes destroyed and lives lost. Residents and volunteers from across the region are working together to clear debris and search for missing persons. The city's water plant was damaged, forcing residents to rely on well water temporarily. Despite the tragedy, the community's resilience and support for one another are evident, with neighbors helping neighbors and volunteers contributing to the recovery efforts.
