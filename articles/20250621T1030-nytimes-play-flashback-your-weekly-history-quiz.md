## Article summary
**"Flashback:** Your Weekly History Quiz, June 21, 2025" presents a variety of engaging topics related to education, health, finance, and language. It highlights the challenges boys face in school due to rising kindergarten expectations, which disproportionately affect them as they tend to mature later. The page also offers an interactive simulation to explore the necessary vaccination rates to control the spread of infectious diseases. Additionally, it raises awareness about the lack of fiduciary duty among banks, questioning where the boundary lies between unethical practices and illegality regarding customer confusion over interest rates. Moreover, it notes that despite improvements in official flight statistics, airlines are extending flight times, making air travel slower in America. Lastly, a dialect quiz invites readers to discover how their speech patterns reflect their geographic origins. The content combines historical insights with contemporary issues, encouraging readers to engage with various aspects of society.
