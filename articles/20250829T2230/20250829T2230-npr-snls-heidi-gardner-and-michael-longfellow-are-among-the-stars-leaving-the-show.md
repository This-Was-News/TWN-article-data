## Article summary
Several cast members and writers of *Saturday Night Live* (SNL) have announced their departures ahead of the show's 51st season. Heidi Gardner, the longest-running female cast member, is leaving after notable performances and memorable characters. Michael Longfellow, Devon Walker, and Eml Wakm also confirmed their exits, expressing gratitude for their time on the show. Writer Celeste Ym, the first out trans person to write for SNL, is leaving after five seasons. Lorne Michaels, the show's creator, hinted at changes to shake things up after the 50th anniversary season. James Austin Johnson will remain to continue his Trump impersonations. SNL is set to return on October 4 without these familiar faces, with potential new hires and departures to be announced in the coming weeks.
