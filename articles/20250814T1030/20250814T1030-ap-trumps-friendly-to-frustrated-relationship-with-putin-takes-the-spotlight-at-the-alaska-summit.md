## Article summary
Donald Trump's summit with Vladimir Putin in Alaska is a pivotal moment for the Ukraine war and Trump's relationship with Putin. Trump has praised Putin's intelligence but recently expressed frustration and threatened more sanctions. The summit is seen as a chance for Putin to repair relations with the West, while critics argue it gives Putin an opportunity to influence Trump. Trump's history with Putin includes doubts about Russian interference in the 2016 election and meetings during his first term, including a controversial summit in Helsinki. Trump has described Putin's invasion of Ukraine as smart but has also expressed frustration with Putin's actions. Putin has sought a summit to improve U.S.-Russia relations and discuss economic projects. Trump's recent actions, including ordering the repositioning of nuclear submarines, reflect his complex relationship with Putin.
