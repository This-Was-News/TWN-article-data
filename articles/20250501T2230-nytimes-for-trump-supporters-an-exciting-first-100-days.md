## Article summary
The article discusses the mixed reactions to President Trump's first 100 days in his second term, highlighting that while 42% of Americans view his presidency as exciting, he has a lower overall favorability rating compared to modern presidents. Supporters express enthusiasm, noting improvements in his approach compared to his first term, with many praising his chosen staff and rapid policy implementation. Some supporters, however, suggest he may need to moderate his pace and express concerns about specific policies, such as dismantling the Department of Education. Trump's business background is cited as a key factor in his appeal, with supporters viewing him as a skilled deal-maker.
