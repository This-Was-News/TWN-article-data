## Article summary
The article titled "Readers Choose Their Top Movies of the 21st Century" highlights the results of a poll where New York Times readers voted on their favorite films from the 21st century, following an official list created by industry professionals. Over 200,000 ballots were cast, revealing a diverse array of films, including blockbusters like "Barbie" and "Dune," alongside international hits such as "Drive My Car" and "The Handmaiden." The list showcases a mix of genres and styles, with titles like "Midsommar" and "Mean Girls" making an appearance, while others like "Mulholland Drive" and "The Social Network" maintained their positions from the original rankings.

Readers are encouraged to explore the list, save films they wish to watch, and even create their own ballots for discussion. Additionally, the article features a comprehensive list of the next 400 films ranked by readers, offering a wealth of cinematic options.
