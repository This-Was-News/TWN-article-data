## Article summary
Vice President JD Vance expressed his loyalty to President Trump while also acknowledging Elon Musk's frustrations regarding Trump's budget bill. During a podcast with comedian Theo Von, Vance praised Musk as an "incredible entrepreneur" and described Musk's criticisms as "understandable." The comments come amid escalating tensions between Trump and Musk, with Musk criticizing the Republican budget for its potential to increase national debt and suggesting the need for a new political party. Trump defended his budget plan on social media, dismissing Musk's criticisms and indicating that Musk should have spoken out earlier. Vance recognized the challenges of navigating Washington's processes from a business perspective, but remained proud to support Trump. He expressed concern about Musk's public attacks on Trump, which he felt could be damaging to both their reputations. Vance also refuted claims linking Trump to Jeffrey Epstein, calling such allegations "total B.S." Overall, he suggested that the feud between Musk and Trump could have negative consequences for the country and for Musk himself.
