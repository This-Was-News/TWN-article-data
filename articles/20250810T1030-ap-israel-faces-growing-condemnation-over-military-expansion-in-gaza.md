## Article summary
Israel faces growing international condemnation over its military expansion in Gaza, with multiple countries rejecting the large-scale operation. Ceasefire efforts are reviving, with mediators Egypt and Qatar preparing a new framework for hostage release and Israeli withdrawal. Health officials report deaths due to malnutrition and violence against Palestinians seeking aid. Hostage families pressure Israel's government, and protests occur both in Israel and internationally. The U.N. Security Council plans an emergency meeting. Israel denies opening fire on aid seekers, but witnesses report otherwise. Aid organizations criticize airdrops as insufficient and dangerous. Gaza faces extreme heat and malnutrition, with families struggling to survive. The death toll from malnutrition and war continues to rise, with disputed figures from both sides. The situation in Gaza is described as catastrophic, with calls for increased humanitarian aid.
