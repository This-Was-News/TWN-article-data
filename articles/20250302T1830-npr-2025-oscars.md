## Article summary
The 2025 Oscars red carpet event is taking place, showcasing a variety of stars as they arrive for the ceremony. The 97th Academy Awards will begin at 7 p.m. EST and 4 p.m. PST, hosted by Conan O'Brien. Notable performances are expected from Ariana Grande and Cynthia Erivo. Leading the nominations is Netflix's musical *Emilia Pérez* with 13 nominations, followed by *The Brutalist* and *Wicked*, each with 10 nominations, including for Best Picture. The event is being broadcast live on ABC and Hulu. Featured appearances include celebrities such as Timothée Chalamet, Jeff Goldblum, and Storm Reid, among others. The atmosphere is vibrant as stars make their way to the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood, dressed in stunning outfits. The page captures the excitement and glamour of the Oscars, highlighting the significance of the event in the film industry. Overall, the anticipation for the awards and performances adds to the allure of the evening.
