## Article summary
Dana, a mother, shares her son Ellott's harrowing experience with predatory online networks that targeted him when he was 14. These networks, such as 764 and CVLT, manipulate vulnerable individuals, especially minors, into harmful behaviors, including self-harm and violence. Dana noticed disturbing changes in Ellott's behavior, including self-harm, withdrawal, and violent thoughts, before he was hospitalized. The FBI has warned about these networks, which have been linked to various crimes, including school shootings, arson, and even terrorism. Dana's story highlights the dangers of these online networks and the importance of awareness and intervention to protect children from their harmful influence.
