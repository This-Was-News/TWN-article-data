## Article summary
Chinese President Xi Jinping is set to host a security summit in China, attended by leaders from Russia and India, including President Vladimir Putin and Prime Minister Narendra Modi. This summit comes at a favorable time for Xi, as India and Russia are both seeking closer ties with China due to their strained relations with the U.S. The summit, followed by a military parade in Beijing showcasing China's newest missiles and warplanes, is a strategic move by Xi to reshape the global order dominated by the United States. The event highlights Xi's efforts to use diplomacy, military might, and historical alliances to enhance China's global influence. The success of Xi's foreign policy is evident in the number of leaders visiting China, signaling a shift in global dynamics. The summit of the Shanghai Cooperation Organization, a Eurasian security group led by China and Russia, will take place in the eastern city of Tianjin.
