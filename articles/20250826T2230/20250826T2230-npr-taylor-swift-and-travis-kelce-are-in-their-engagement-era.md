## Article summary
Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, both 35, are engaged after dating for about two years. They announced the news on Instagram with a post featuring photos of the proposal and a reference to Swift's song 'So High School.' The announcement comes shortly after Swift's latest album release and just before the NFL season. Their relationship began in late 2023 after Kelce attended one of her concerts. Swift has been spotted at Chiefs games, and Kelce has made surprise appearances at her concerts. Their relationship has garnered significant attention from fans and has boosted NFL viewership among women. Swift spoke publicly about their relationship in a Time interview, expressing pride in supporting each other's careers. Known for her love songs, Swift's early hit 'Love Story' has become a popular engagement anthem, fittingly mirroring her current situation.
