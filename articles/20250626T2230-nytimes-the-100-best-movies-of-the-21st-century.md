## Article summary
The list of the 100 best movies of the 21st century features films voted on by over 500 influential figures in Hollywood. It highlights a diverse array of genres and storytelling techniques, showcasing notable films such as "Superbad," a teen comedy that combines humor with heartfelt moments, and "Parasite," a Korean police procedural that defies traditional genre conventions. Other standout films include "Gravity," an action-packed survival story set in space, and "Black Panther," which addresses themes of identity and responsibility through its powerful female characters. The document also explores films like "Lady Bird," which captures the complexities of adolescence and mother-daughter relationships, and "Moonlight," a poignant coming-of-age story that examines love and identity. Artistic achievements such as "The Tree of Life" and "Roma" reflect on memory and the human experience, while "Spotlight" offers a gripping look at investigative journalism. The list spans a wide range of styles and themes, illustrating the richness of contemporary cinema and the impact these films have had on audiences and culture. Each film is celebrated not only for its entertainment value but also for its deeper commentary on society and the human condition.
