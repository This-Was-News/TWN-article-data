## Article summary
A shooting occurred near the CDC headquarters and Emory University in Atlanta, resulting in the death of a DeKalb County police officer and the suspected gunman. The attack damaged multiple CDC buildings, with bullet marks visible on windows. Authorities found the gunman dead in a nearby building, but his motive remains unknown. The shooter was armed with a long gun, and three other firearms were recovered. No other injuries were reported, and CDC employees were unharmed. The CDC director announced a remote workday for Monday to assess security. The area, usually busy, was deserted during the incident. Governor Brian Kemp praised the first responders for their bravery. The shooting is part of a developing news story, with updates expected. The emotional impact on the officer's family was highlighted by local officials.
