## Article summary

He expressed his discontent on social media, urging House members who supported the bill to reconsider their actions and hinting at potential political repercussions in the 2026 midterm elections. Musk described the bill as massive and detrimental, claiming it would exacerbate the national budget deficit and harm American interests. His comments signal a growing rift between him and Trump, especially as Musk distances himself from his former governmental role. The legislation, officially titled the One Big, Beautiful Bill Act, proposes tax cuts primarily benefiting the wealthy while reducing funding for health, education, and clean energy programs.
