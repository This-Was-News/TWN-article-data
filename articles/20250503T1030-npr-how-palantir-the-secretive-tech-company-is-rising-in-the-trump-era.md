## Article summary
Palantir, a tech company known for its data-mining capabilities, is gaining prominence in the Trump administration, particularly through contracts related to immigration enforcement. The company's stock valuation has surged significantly, reflecting its expanding role in government operations. CEO Alex Karp emphasizes Palantir's mission to enhance the effectiveness of its partners, even in military contexts, which has drawn both interest and criticism. The firm has contracts with various agencies, including a recent $30 million deal to track migrant movements and a nearly $1 billion contract with the Navy.
