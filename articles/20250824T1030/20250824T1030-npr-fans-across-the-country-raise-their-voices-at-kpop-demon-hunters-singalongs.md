## Article summary
Fans across the country are enthusiastically participating in singalong events for the popular Netflix animated film 'KPop Demon Hunters,' which is being screened in theaters for one weekend only. The film, about an all-girl rock band battling evil spirits, has garnered significant attention and is set to become Netflix's most-watched movie. Screenings are selling out, and several songs from the soundtrack are among the top tracks on Spotify. The event is described as a special gift for fans and a strategic move to promote the film to a broader audience. Families and groups of friends are dressing up as characters from the movie and enjoying the singalong experience. The film's creators are hinting at a potential sequel, suggesting that 'KPop Demon Hunters' could become a major franchise with endless possibilities. Fans are taking selfies, dancing with glow sticks, and enjoying the soundtrack, even those who are not typically K-pop fans. The event highlights the strong connection between the film and its audience.
