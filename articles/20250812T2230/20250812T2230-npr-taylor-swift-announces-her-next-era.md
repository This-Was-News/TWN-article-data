## Article summary
Taylor Swift has announced her 12th studio album, titled 'The Life of a Showgirl'. The revelation came after a series of cryptic social media posts and a countdown clock on her official website. Fans had been eagerly anticipating news of her next project following the conclusion of her record-breaking Eras Tour in December 2024. The album's cover art, tracklist, and release date remain undisclosed, but pre-sales for vinyl, cassette, CD, and poster are available on her website. This will be Swift's first new album since she reacquired the rights to her master recordings in May 2025. The announcement was made on the 'New Heights' podcast, hosted by her boyfriend Travis Kelce and his brother Jason Kelce. The album's title and the consistent use of the color orange in promotional materials have sparked excitement and speculation among fans. Swift is known for embedding Easter eggs and hidden messages in her work, adding to the intrigue surrounding the new album. The release date is yet to be confirmed, with pre-sales items expected to ship before October 13, 2025. This album marks a significant milestone in Swift's career, following her lengthy process of re-recording her first six albums and releasing them as 'Taylor's Version'.
