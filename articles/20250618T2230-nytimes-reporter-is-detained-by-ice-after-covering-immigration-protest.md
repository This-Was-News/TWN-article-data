## Article summary
Mario Guevara, a journalist known for covering immigration issues in Georgia, was detained by U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) after being arrested while reporting on a protest against Trump administration immigration policies. Guevara, originally from El Salvador and living in the U.S. for over 20 years, held a work permit and Social Security number but was not a legal permanent resident or citizen. His legal team plans to challenge his detention, believing he has a defendable case. Eyewitness accounts suggest he was complying with police orders when he was taken into custody.
