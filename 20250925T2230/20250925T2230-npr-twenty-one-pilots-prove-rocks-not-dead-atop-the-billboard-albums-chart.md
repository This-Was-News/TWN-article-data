## Article summary
Twenty One Pilots' album 'Breach' tops the Billboard 200 chart with strong sales and streaming numbers, including a record streaming week for the band. The album's success is driven by high vinyl sales and a debut on the streaming chart, though it lacks a hit single. Ed Sheeran also debuts in the top 10. Meanwhile, the soundtrack hit 'Golden' from 'KPop Demon Hunters' extends its reign at No. 1 on the Hot 100, surpassing 'We Don't Talk About Bruno' from 'Encanto.' Teddy Swims' 'Lose Control' continues its lengthy stay in the top 10. Additionally, Rihanna's 2007 song 'Breakin' Dishes' makes its Hot 100 debut due to TikTok popularity, indicating a resurgence in her music. Despite her recent singles not charting, the success of 'Breakin' Dishes' suggests ongoing interest in her music.
