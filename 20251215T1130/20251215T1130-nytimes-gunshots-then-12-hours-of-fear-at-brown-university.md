## Article summary
A masked man with a rifle entered a lecture hall at Brown University, opening fire on approximately 60 students attending a study session ahead of final exams. The attack resulted in two fatalities and nine injuries. The shooter's motive and identity remain unknown, and authorities conducted a 12-hour search, detaining and later releasing a person of interest without charges. The search for the suspect is ongoing, with law enforcement seeking public assistance and reviewing video footage. Students were initially alerted to the active shooter situation via a phone message, instructing them to shelter in place on the Providence, R.I., campus. The incident caused widespread fear and disruption, particularly as students were preparing for their final exams. Enhanced law enforcement presence has been established on campus and in the surrounding city. The university community is grappling with the aftermath of this violent event.
