## Article summary
Saturday Night Live has announced five new cast members ahead of its 51st season. The new performers include Ben Marshall, who has been promoted from the digital short team 'Please Don't Destroy,' along with Tommy Brennan, Jeremy Culhane, Kam Patterson, and Veronka Slowkowska. Ben Marshall, known for his work with Please Don't Destroy, will now join the main cast. The announcement comes after several cast members and writers, including Hed Gardner, announced their departures. Lorne Michaels, the show's creator, had hinted at a post-50th anniversary shakeup. The new season is set to premiere on October 4. The new cast members bring diverse backgrounds, including stand-up comedy, acting, and digital content creation. Each member has shared their excitement and gratitude on social media platforms. Veronka Slowkowska, a Canadian actress and comedian, is notable for her viral TikTok and Instagram content. The new additions aim to bring fresh talent and energy to the iconic late-night show.
