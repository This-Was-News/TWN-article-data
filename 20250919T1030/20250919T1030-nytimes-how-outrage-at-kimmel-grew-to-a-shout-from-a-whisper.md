## Article summary
Outrage over Jimmy Kimmel's monologue escalated from a whisper to a shout, fueled by right-wing social media users, influencers, and news outlets. The initial reaction was muted, but it gained traction as conservative figures and Fox News hosts amplified the clip. Elon Musk's post calling Kimmel 'disgusting' further intensified the furor. The outrage was orchestrated by influential figures and platforms, reflecting how online outrage often spreads through prominent voices. Critics accused Kimmel of inaccurately linking the shooter to the MAGA movement. The controversy led ABC to pull Kimmel's show indefinitely. The Media Research Center, a conservative watchdog, has a history of campaigning against mainstream media. Fox News hosts discussed the potential for ABC to cancel Kimmel's show. The F.C.C. chairman's suggestion of regulatory action propelled the outrage into the mainstream. The episode highlights how influential figures and algorithms drive online outrage, leading to real-world consequences.
