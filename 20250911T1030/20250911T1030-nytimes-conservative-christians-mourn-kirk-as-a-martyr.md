## Article summary
Conservative Christians are mourning Charles Kirk as a martyr following his assassination. Kirk, the founder of Turning Point USA, was seen as a symbol of hope and a bridge between right-wing politics and evangelical faith. His death has deeply impacted many, with evangelical pastors, activists, and young conservatives feeling his loss personally. Jackson Lahmeyer, a pastor in Oklahoma, described Kirk as a friend and a martyr who died for his beliefs. The assassination is viewed by many as an attack on conservative Christian values. Various Christian groups and leaders, including President Trump and Vice President JD Vance, have expressed their condolences and highlighted Kirk's influence. Kirk's legacy includes encouraging young conservative men to embrace their values and family life. He transitioned from a secular provocateur to a prominent evangelical figure during the COVID-19 pandemic. Kirk's death is seen as a sign that the spiritual battle for conservative Christians is far from over, with leaders like Shane Winnngs vowing to continue his mission.
