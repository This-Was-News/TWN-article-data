## Article summary
The Nobel Prize in literature will be announced on Thursday, with the winner joining notable laureates like Ernest Hemingway, Toni Morrison, and Kazuo Ishiguro. The prize has been awarded 117 times to 121 winners by the Swedish Academy. Last year's winner was South Korean author Han Kang for her work confronting historical traumas. The literature prize is the fourth Nobel announced this week, following medicine, physics, and chemistry. The Nobel Peace Prize will be announced on Friday, with U.S. President Donald Trump considered a long shot. The Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences will be announced on Monday. Award ceremonies are held on December 10, the anniversary of Alfred Nobel's death. Each prize includes 11 million Swedish kronor, a gold medal, and a diploma. Nobel was a Swedish industrialist and inventor of dynamite who founded the prizes.
