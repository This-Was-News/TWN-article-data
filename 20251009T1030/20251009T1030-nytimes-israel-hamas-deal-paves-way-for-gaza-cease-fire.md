## Article summary
Israel and Hamas have agreed on initial terms for a cease-fire, aiming to end their two-year war. The deal includes a hostage exchange and aid delivery to Gaza. President Trump and Arab mediators played key roles in the negotiations. The agreement marks the start of a formal cease-fire, with Israel preparing to pull back troops. Hamas urges full implementation of the deal. Critical issues like postwar Gaza governance remain unresolved. Gaza faces severe food shortages, with aid workers hopeful for increased supplies. Israeli military operations continue despite the impending cease-fire. The war has caused significant casualties and destruction. Palestinians and Israelis express hope and uncertainty about the deal's impact. The agreement follows sustained international pressure and diplomatic efforts.
