## Article summary
Arjun Malaviya, a 19-year-old, has visited 118 countries, becoming the youngest solo traveler to reach 100 countries. He started his journey at 17, aiming to connect with locals and experience diverse cultures. His travels included remote islands, bustling cities, and even the DMZ. Malaviya highlighted the similarities among people worldwide, despite cultural differences. He navigated challenges, such as a harrowing experience in Odessa during the Ukraine-Russia war. His trip was self-funded, and he planned meticulously to ensure safety and communication with his parents. Malaviya's journey was inspired by the isolation of the COVID pandemic, and he hopes to inspire others to embrace new experiences and cultures.
