## Article summary
Democratic Sen. Mark Kelly of Arizona stated that Donald Trump does not "understand the Constitution." This statement comes in the context of Kelly, a Navy veteran and former astronaut, appearing in a video with other Democratic lawmakers. In the video, they informed active-duty troops that they are not obligated to follow illegal orders. Kelly's remarks were made during an interview with NPR's Scott Detrow. The interview took place amidst potential threats of a military court martial and FBI inquiries related to the video. The episode of "Consider This from NPR" where this interview is featured is approximately 15 minutes and 22 seconds long.
