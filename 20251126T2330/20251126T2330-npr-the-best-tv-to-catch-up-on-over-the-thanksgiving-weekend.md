## Article summary
This Thanksgiving, catch up on some of the best TV shows of the year, offering a diverse range of genres for every viewer. Sci-fi fans can dive into "Alien: Earth," a thrilling exploration of the franchise's premise on Earth, or "Pluribus," a creative story about a novelist isolated by an alien virus. For mystery and noir enthusiasts, "The Lowdown" offers a street-wise tale set in Tulsa, while "The Beast In Me" presents a compelling cat-and-mouse drama starring Claire Danes. Political drama and relationship complexities unfold in "The Diplomat" Season 3, featuring Keri Russell and Allison Janney. Spy thriller aficionados will enjoy "Slow Horses" Season 5, with Gary Oldman returning as the cunning Jackson Lamb. "Being Eddie" offers a unique look into Eddie Murphy's life and career, and Ken Burns' "The American Revolution" provides a detailed historical account. Music lovers can explore Rick Beato's insightful YouTube videos on musical craft. Finally, for those seeking a classic binge, "Game of Thrones" remains a relevant, albeit cautionary, tale of power and its consequences, offering parallels to modern political landscapes.
