## Article summary
The New York Times conducted a nearly two-hour exclusive interview with President Trump in the Oval Office. The conversation covered a wide range of topics, including the fatal ICE shooting in Minneapolis, immigration, U.S. strategy in Venezuela, the Russia-Ukraine war, Greenland and NATO, his health, and plans for White House renovations. During the interview, Trump also took an off-the-record call from Gustavo Petro, the president of Colombia. At the conclusion of the discussion, Trump gave the reporters a tour of the White House residence. The New York Times plans to release further coverage of the interview in the form of stories, newsletters, videos, and an episode of The Daily. A full transcript of the interview will also be published.
