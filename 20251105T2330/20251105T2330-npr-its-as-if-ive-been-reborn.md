## Article summary
Misty Copeland, the first Black female principal dancer at American Ballet Theatre, has taken her final bow at Lincoln Center after a career milestone. Copeland views her body's changes, especially after motherhood, as a rebirth, allowing her to re-engage with ballet technique in a new way.
