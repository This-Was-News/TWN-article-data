## Article summary
President Donald Trump announced via social media that the U.S. conducted a large-scale strike in Venezuela overnight, resulting in the capture of Venezuelan President Nicolas Maduro and his wife. Trump stated they have been transported out of the country, with the operation involving U.S. Law Enforcement. Secretary of State Marco Rubio has previously asserted that Maduro is not the legitimate president of Venezuela and leads the Cartel de Los Soles, an organization indicted for drug trafficking into the U.S. A news conference was scheduled for Saturday morning. This is a developing story.
