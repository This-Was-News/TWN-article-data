## Article summary
A shooting at Brown University on Saturday afternoon resulted in the deaths of two students and injuries to nine others. A person of interest, a 24-year-old man from Wisconsin, was taken into custody on Sunday at a hotel in Coventry, Rhode Island. Authorities have not yet filed charges or released information regarding a motive for the attack. The shooting occurred during final exams, leading to the cancellation of all remaining classes and academic activities for the semester. Students described hiding under desks and in locked rooms as police searched the campus for the shooter. Two handguns and loaded magazines were recovered when the person of interest was apprehended. Among the injured, one student had been released from the hospital, while others remained in critical condition. The university community is coming together to support those affected and to remember the victims. The incident has disrupted the academic calendar and caused significant distress among students and faculty.
