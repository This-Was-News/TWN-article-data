## Article summary
A bystander is being hailed as a hero for tackling and disarming one of the gunmen who attacked a Hanukkah celebration at Bondi Beach in Sydney, Australia. Verified video footage shows the unarmed man approaching one of the shooters from behind and wrestling a long gun away from him. The premier of New South Wales, Chris Minns, described the man as a genuine hero, stating that many lives were saved due to his bravery. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese of Australia also commended civilians who risked their lives to help others, characterizing their actions as embodying the Australian spirit of standing up for values. Minns expressed disbelief at the scene, calling it unbelievable and highlighting the individual's self-risk to save others. Former President Trump also praised the man as very brave, noting that he saved many lives.
