## Article summary
A man is being hailed as a hero for disarming one of the gunmen during a deadly attack at Sydney's Bondi Beach. Video footage shows the unarmed civilian tackling and disarming the gunman, potentially saving numerous lives. The New South Wales Premier praised the man's "unbelievable" actions, stating that many people are alive tonight because of his bravery. Reports identify the man as Ahmed al Ahmed, a 43-year-old fruit shop owner, who was reportedly shot twice and is hospitalized. The attack, which occurred during a Hanukkah celebration, resulted in at least 15 deaths and 42 hospitalizations. Authorities are investigating the incident, with footage also showing other gunmen firing from a footbridge.
